Server Rules

WitchyWorlds thrives when everyone treats each other with respect and plays fair. Please read and follow the guidelines below so our witchy-cottage community at witchyworlds.top stays welcoming, safe, and lag-free.

Community Expectations

Respect & Conduct

  • Be respectful to all players.
  • No harassment, racism, sexism, or hate speech.
  • English in public chat only (swearing is allowed, but keep it minimal and SFW).
  • No spamming, trolling, or advertising other servers.
  • Don’t impersonate staff or other players.
  • Follow staff directions at all times.

Fair Play

  • No exploiting, duping, x-raying, or cheating mods.
  • Keep trades and giveaways fair (don’t ruin progression).
  • Don’t exploit bugs; if you find one, report it so we can fix it.
  • Use common sense — if you think it might be against the rules, ask staff first.

Item & Build Limits

  • Claim land with FTB Chunks only.
  • Max 32 Soul Surges per team, max 1 per block face.
  • Max 6 spawners per team; all farms need an off switch.
  • No more than 64 of the same machine type per team.
  • Max 1 quarry per team, and only used in the mining dimension.
  • Don’t bypass pack spawn limits or chunk caps.
  • No chunkloader blocks/items (FTB Chunks only).
  • No Ultimate Pipe Upgrades (Pipez Infinity Upgrade).
  • No AE2 ↔ Sophisticated Storage or RS external storage cable.
  • No Chunk Destroyer.
  • Keep bases compact; avoid laggy/redstone spam.
  • Staff may remove laggy setups or enforce limits.
